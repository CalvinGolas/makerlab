Welcome to the Maker Lab!

The place for all of your digital design needs! Feel free to pop by anytime during lab assistant hours if you ever want to learn the basics of 3d printing or digital drawing. We also host Maker Mondays every so often: workshops geared towards a specific type of design!

The lab features cutting-edge tools for digital drawing and painting, 2-D and 3-D animation and modeling, laser and CNC spindle router cutting, 3-D printing and scanning, and a wide array of hand tools, electronics, and other equipment for creative projects and experimentation.
